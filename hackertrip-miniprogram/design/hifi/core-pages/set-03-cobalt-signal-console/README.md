## Reference

- Microsoft Fluent 2 - Color: https://fluent2.microsoft.design/color

## Why This Direction

Fluent's color guidance separates neutral surfaces, brand colors, and semantic colors. This fits HackerTrip because the product needs a clean AI workspace with clear action priority and deadline signals.

## Product Logic

- Cobalt blue drives navigation, selected chips, progress, and primary CTA.
- Signal orange marks countdown, heat, and urgency.
- White and titanium surfaces keep the page lightweight.
- The palette is familiar enough for productivity tools but still modern.

## Fit For HackerTrip

Best for a polished, scalable mini-program that can grow from event discovery into schedule management, identity cards, and project workspaces.

## Risk

Blue-white AI products are common. The art direction needs strong event covers and HackerTrip copy to avoid feeling generic.

## Recommendation

Recommended as one of the top two options because it balances trust, scalability, and technical clarity.
