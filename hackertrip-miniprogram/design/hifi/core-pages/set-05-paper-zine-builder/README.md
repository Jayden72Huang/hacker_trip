## Why This Direction

Ant Design's color model provides structured product palettes while leaving room for product-level tone. Paper Zine Builder uses that logic for a maker-community feel: warm paper, black ink, orange action, and poster-like event cards.

## Product Logic

- Warm paper background makes the app feel community-driven and creative.
- Orange represents builder energy and primary actions.
- Red marks deadline pressure.
- Event cards become poster artifacts, which suits hackathon culture.

## Fit For HackerTrip

Best for community, content, and creator-oriented positioning. It makes HackerTrip feel like a curated hackathon zine plus AI assistant.

## Risk

It is less "AI platform" and more editorial/community. Enterprise teams or serious AI builders may read it as less technical.

## Recommendation

Use this if the brand wants to lean into community, storytelling, and event curation.
