## Why This Direction

TDesign and WeChat mini-program conventions prioritize practical mobile density, clear touch targets, and component consistency. Mint Hardware Lab applies that discipline to a hardware/robotics innovation theme.

## Product Logic

- White and soft mint keep the app native and light.
- Teal marks progress, active states, and primary actions.
- Copper is used only for countdown/heat.
- Hardware imagery makes AI, robotics, and maker tracks feel tangible.

## Fit For HackerTrip

Best for a WeChat-native product that needs to feel clean, credible, and useful across AI, hardware, and product competitions.

## Risk

The palette may feel more hardware-specific than general hackathon discovery. If the product covers many software-only competitions, covers and copy need to broaden the theme.

## Recommendation

Use this if Tencent mini-program fit and hardware/AI innovation are priority signals.
